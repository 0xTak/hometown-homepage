Visit Boston

Welcome to a charming and historic city with a rich cultural heritage, world-class museums, and a vibrant food and nightlife scene.

Top three activities to do in Boston

Walk the Freedom Trail

This 2.5-mile long trail takes you through 16 historically significant sites in Boston, including the Old North Church, Paul Revere's House, and the USS Constitution Museum.

Visit the Boston Tea Party Museum

Learn about the events leading up to the famous Boston Tea Party and participate in an interactive experience that brings the event to life.

Explore the Museum of Fine Arts

This museum is home to over 450,000 works of art from around the world, including pieces by Monet, Van Gogh, and Degas.
